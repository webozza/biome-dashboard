1. How to sign in

  1. Open Chrome or Safari.
  2. Go to http://localhost:3000.
  3. Type the email and password shown below.
  4. Click the green Sign in button.

2. What you see on the screen

  • Left side: menu of all sections
  • Top-right circle: your profile (click to sign out)
  • Middle: whatever page you opened

3. Your daily routine

Every day you follow the same 5 small steps: sign in → open section → pick a row → read details → decide.

4. Approve a Verification

A Verification is when a user says "please give me a blue tick."

  1. Click Verification on the left menu.
  2. Find a row with Pending.
  3. Click that row — a side panel opens.
  4. Read the details (their social account, reason, proof).
  5. Click Approve if real, or Reject if fake.

4b. BMID Content — approve a post made inside Biome

BMID Content is for posts made inside the Biome app that want the blue BMID stamp. (BMID Box is for posts from outside — that is Section 5.)

  1. Click BMID Content on the left menu.
  2. In the Requests tab, find a row marked Pending or In Review.
  3. Click the row — a side panel opens.
  4. Read the post title, preview image, and reason.
  5. Click Approve → voting opens. Or Reject → it ends here.
  6. Check Voting tab later to see the community result.
Own vs Duality:
Own — the user owns the post alone.
Duality — the post is shared with a tagged user. That tagged user must accept first before you see it in Admin Review.

5. BMID Box — the full journey of a post

A BMID Box request goes through four main stages. The diagram shows the full path.

  1. Click BMID Box on the menu.
  2. Find a row with Admin Review.
  3. Click the row — the detail page opens.
  4. Click Open Voting to let the community vote, or Reject to stop it now.
  5. After voting ends, the result becomes Approved or Refused.

6. Moderation — handle a report

  1. Click Moderation on the menu.
  2. Pick the Pending tab.
  3. Click a report to read why it was submitted.
  4. Choose one:
    • Dismiss — the report is not a real problem.
    • Actioned — you did something about it.
    • Delete content — the content must go away.

7. Select many, act once

  1. Tick the checkboxes on the left of the rows you want.
  2. Green buttons appear at the top (Approve / Delete).
  3. Click the button — a popup asks "Are you sure?"
  4. Click Confirm. Done!
Tip: tick the checkbox in the table header to select every row on the current page at once.

8. Delete one row

  1. Look at the far right of the row — you see a trash icon.
  2. Click it. A popup asks you to confirm.
  3. Click Delete. Gone.
Careful: most deletes cannot be undone. Read the row before you click.

10. If something breaks

ProblemWhat to do
Page looks brokenRefresh (press F5 or Cmd+R)
Cannot sign inCheck password. Your account may not have admin rights.
Button does nothingWait 2 seconds, then try again.
Red error shows upRead the message — it usually tells you what to fix.
Still stuckTake a screenshot and ask a senior admin.
